Frequently asked questions.

You can always contact us to find out more.

How do we get started?

Contact us and let us know what types of problems you're facing. If it's a match with our skills, we will schedule a free consult to determine the best way we can work with you.

What is the pricing model?

We are flexible depending on the project. For projects with clear deliverables, we can estimate a single payment for services. Embedding us into long-running projects, however, will likely be billed hourly.

Why maintain open source projects?

We love contributing to the open source community. We also regularly use our open source work with clients, especially for database performance and design. Our most commonly used libraries are django-pgtrigger, django-pghistory, and django-pgbulk.

How many people will work with me?

Almost all of the time it will just be one of us working with you. This is determined based on what problems you face, along with the time zone of your team.
